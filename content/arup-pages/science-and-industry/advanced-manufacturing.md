               <p>Manufacturing in 2050 will look very different from today, and will be virtually unrecognisable from that of 30 years ago. 
</p><P>
Successful businesses will be able to rapidly adapt their assets and intelligence in response to a faster-paced, changing global market. Embracing sustainability, innovation and skills whilst improving margins is key in remaining competitive and meeting stakeholder expectations.  Increasingly our clients’ key challenges are focused upon creating value from estates, delivering high quality products, continually improving performance, creating responsible strategies, demonstrating value and total people commitment.
</P><P>
Arup offers a complete range of industrial engineering competencies and technical skills, providing a holistic approach to planning, engineering design and project delivery.  By designing from the inside out, we ensure the most effective relationship between the people, processes and surrounding building environment. We actively ensure both flexibility and agility are considered to ensure the overall longevity of an asset.
</P>
                </p>

               <h2>Advanced manufacturing</h2><p>
Manufacturing has entered a new age of production. This shift in the design and manufacture of goods is not the result of a single trend, but is driven by a broad range of complex and interconnected factors including the growing impact of technology on the workforce and process optimisation through big data and AI.
</p>
